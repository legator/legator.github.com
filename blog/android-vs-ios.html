Another thought about Android vs iOS
← Back

You can find a lot of stories/posts/threads about comparisons Android and iOS, but this story about how to configure smart home on IOS or Android. My the first phone was Nokia with Symbion OS - in that time we knew nothing about smart home or iot, but on another hand - it was a really good smartphone. The next phone was Nexus 4 - the best Android phone made by LG in that time. And less then one year ago I bought iPhone SE. But the next my phone will be on Android. So, there are reasons:
  • Android OS is opened for developers. I am a developer and it gives me to write needed applications or services. I don't want to buy developer licenses or something like this. I want to create applications only for me only)) On Android I have access to the battery, call etc and share status it with other applications. I use several rules on IFTTT for Android device but with my iPhone it is not working.
  • I really like Chromecast. I don't have smart TV or tv box and Chromecast is the best solution in this situation. But I have a problem with connection iPhone to Cast: cannot share a screen, cannot share browser, there are no good players for iOS. iOS only support Apple TV but there are a lot of tvs with build-in Chromecast. And yes, Apple will be ignored this technology.
  • There is no good app for IPTV. My internet providers provide IPTV service, but on my iPhone this is unused.
  • I like such program as xmusic match, messager from Facebook etc with it notifications and additional views. But on iPhone it is absent.
  • I am using Google services and it is useful to log in once in a device and then use this account in another app.
  • NFC and pay - it is a really bad situation in IOS. I have several headphones, speakers with NFC - but you cannot use it with your iPhone and you cannot use Google Pay etc - you need to wait for Apple Pay((
  • Google Assistant more "clever" then Siri.
  • One thinks that I like on iOS is that new version of iOS supported different versions of iPhone, because on Android - new phone will be supported during next two years.
In my mind, people which use iPhone did not know about all benefits that Android gives. They use the iPhone to emphasize their status, but really revolutions made by Samsung, Xiaomi, Google.
